<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="About_Tiers">About Tiers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/dota2/index.php?title=Portal:Tournaments&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: About Tiers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3 and Tier 4 are the tiers for the most important tournaments on this wiki. They are categorized as follows:
</p>
<table class="rl-responsive-table" style="width:95%;">
<tbody><tr><th scope="col" style="width:10%">Name</th><th scope="col" style="width:10%">Typical prize pool</th><th scope="col">Description</th></tr>
<tr><td><b><a href="/dota2/Tier_1_Tournaments" title="Tier 1 Tournaments">Tier 1</a></b></td><td>$300,000+</td><td class="rl-responsive-table-left-align">Former <i>Premier</i>. <b>Tier 1 Tournaments</b> offer an outstanding prize pool, are almost exclusively played offline, and feature the best teams from all over the world.</td></tr>
<tr><td><b><a href="/dota2/Tier_2_Tournaments" class="mw-redirect" title="Tier 2 Tournaments">Tier 2</a></b></td><td>$50,000+</td><td class="rl-responsive-table-left-align">Former <i>Major</i>. <b>Tier 2 Tournaments</b> feature a large prize pool and a good number of top-tier teams.</td></tr>
<tr><td><b><a href="/dota2/Tier_3_Tournaments" class="mw-redirect" title="Tier 3 Tournaments">Tier 3</a></b></td><td>$3,000+</td><td class="rl-responsive-table-left-align">Former <i>Minor</i>. <b>Tier 3 Tournaments</b> offer a smaller prize pool and less prestige than <b>Tier 2 Tournaments</b> but still draw a high level of competition.</td></tr>
<tr><td><b><a href="/dota2/Tier_4_Tournaments" title="Tier 4 Tournaments">Tier 4</a></b></td><td>$3,000 or less</td><td class="rl-responsive-table-left-align"><b>Tier 4 Tournaments</b> are the lowest ranked events, almost always played online with no top teams participating.</td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<p><i>While the prize pool is an important factor for categorizing tournaments, it does not equal a specific tier.</i>
</p>
