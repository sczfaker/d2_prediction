<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Format">Format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/dota2/index.php?title=Oceanic_Esports_League/2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b>Participants</b>
<ul><li>Ten invited teams</li></ul></li>
<li><b>Group Stage</b> - <i>August 5th - October 17th, 2020</i>
<ul><li>Double round-robin</li>
<li>All matches are <abbr title="Best-of-two">Bo2</abbr></li>
<li><span style="background-color:rgb(221,244,221);">Top four teams</span> advance to the upper bracket of the Playoffs</li>
<li><span style="background-color:rgb(249,240,199);">5<sup>th</sup> and 6<sup>th</sup> place teams</span> advance to the lower bracket of the Playoffs</li>
<li><span style="background-color:rgb(251,223,223);">Bottom four teams</span> are eliminated</li></ul></li>
<li><b>Playoffs</b> - <i>October 20th - October 24th, 2020</i>
<ul><li>Double-elimination bracket</li>
<li>Grand Final is <abbr title="Best-of-five">Bo5</abbr>, all other matches are <abbr title="Best-of-three">Bo3</abbr></li></ul></li></ul>
